Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about DevConf 2026.

Who should attend DevConf 2026?

DevConf is built for software engineers, developers, students, system architects, and tech founders looking to explore the cutting edge of AI, Cloud architecture, and development paradigms.

Will certificates be provided for the workshops?

Yes! All attendees participating in the hands-on tracks will receive official digital certificates of completion signed by the track speakers.

Can I switch tracks midway through the conference?

Absolutely. Your pass gives you open access to all keynotes and presentation rooms, though workshop seating is first-come, first-served.

Are the ticket prices refundable?

Tickets are fully refundable up to 14 days before the event kick-off date. Alternatively, you can transfer your ticket to a peer free of charge.
